STUDENT NOTES SHARING SYSTEM

A Student Notes Sharing System is a web-based platform designed to allow students to upload, 
share, search, and download academic materials in a centralized and organized manner. The system 
enhances collaborative learning by enabling students to access notes, past papers, and study resources 
uploaded by their peers across different courses and academic levels.

Registered users can upload notes by providing details such as course name, 
subject, description, and file type (PDF, DOC, etc.). The system stores these 
resources securely and makes them available for other users to view or download. 
Users can search for notes using keywords, course codes, or categories, making it
easier to find relevant study materials quickly.

The platform also supports user authentication, ensuring that only authorized users can upload or manage content.
Different user roles such as students and administrators are maintained. Students can upload, download, 
and rate notes, while administrators can review, approve, or remove inappropriate or duplicate content to 
maintain quality standards.

Additionally, the system keeps track of uploaded files, downloads, and user activity for accountability and 
analytics. It may include features such as ratings, comments, and recommendations to highlight useful materials.
Notifications can be implemented to inform users about newly uploaded notes in their courses or updates to existing
materials.

Overall, the system improves access to educational resources, promotes knowledge sharing, 
reduces duplication of effort, and supports academic success through a collaborative digital 
environment.
